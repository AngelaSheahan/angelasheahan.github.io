Recommended FUN games

Fall Guys

'Fall Guys' involves up to 60 players who control jellybean-like creatures and compete against each other in a series of randomly selected fun mini-games. Players are eliminated as the rounds progress until, eventually, the last remaining player is crowned the winner.

Gang Beasts

Gang Beasts is a multiplayer 'play-fight'' party game with gelatinous and rather floppy characters, melee fight sequences, and hazardous environments, set in the fictional metropolis of Beef City.

Mario Party Superstars

'Mario Party Superstars' features gameplay similar to the first eight entries in the Mario Party series. Four characters, played by either humans or AI, traverse one of five boards in the game, collecting coins and stars. The player with the most stars at the end of the game wins
